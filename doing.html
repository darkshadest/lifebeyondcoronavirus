Things we are doing in response to this situation

Many things seem to be going crazy because of the coronavirus, but we have gotten over these problems and have done whatever we can to get life to the most normal form we can. Here are a few things we people have done to get life into a smooth flow and keep ourselves busy.

  • Many school districts are attempting Virtual-Learning, or in other words, school through the computer
  • We are doing online extracurricular classes
  • We have taken time to practice skills and are becoming better and better
  • Companies have hired robot workers so that they can keep workers safe
  • We are recognizing the real potential of the Internet because now everything is happening through the internet
  • People are using time at home to connect with family, near and far(virtually)
